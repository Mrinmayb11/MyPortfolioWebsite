  • What is the cost of the website?

    The price depends on the complexity of the project. Once I evaluate the functionality, needs, and scale of the project, I will be able to give you the exact cost.

  • How long will it take to get a new website?

    For a standard website, the typical timeline is 2-4 weeks. However, this can vary depending on project size, complexity, and how quickly you provide feedback

  • What if I am not satisfied with the results?

    Client satisfaction is my top priority. If you're not completely happy with the results, I offer unlimited revisions to address your concerns. If despite my best efforts i cannot reach a satisfactory outcome, I will refund the payments excluding the initial deposit.

  • Do you offer website maintenance services or training?

    Yes, I offer post-launch support to ensure your website's success. After the website is live, I'll create a personalized Loom video tutorial on how to manage basic website content. if needed, I also offer website maintenance services on a monthly subscription basis.

  • What is your payment structure?

    My standard payment structure is, 50% deposit to initiate the project and the rest 50% upon website launch and delivery. For larger projects, 30% deposit to initiate the project, 30% upon design approval and the rest 40% upon development completion and website launch.
